✈️ Pressure Altitude Calculator

Calculate pressure altitude for flight planning and performance

Hectopascals (hPa)
Inches Hg (inHg)

Calculation Method:

Pressure Altitude (ft) = (1013.25 - Current Pressure hPa) × 30

Standard Sea Level Pressure: 1013.25 hPa (29.92 inHg)

Conversion Factor: ~30 feet per hectopascal

Positive values indicate altitude above sea level, negative values indicate below sea level in standard atmosphere.
